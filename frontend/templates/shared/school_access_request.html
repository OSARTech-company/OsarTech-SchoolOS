How Onboarding Works

Step 1 Submit your school details

Provide the school profile, official contacts, and proof document once.

Step 2 Verify the school email

Use the 6-digit code sent after submission to enter the review queue.

Step 3 Manual verification

The super admin confirms phone and document checks before approval.

Step 4 Approval and invite

Once approved, the proposed admin receives an account setup invite.
